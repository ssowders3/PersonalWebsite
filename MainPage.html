Discover East

Discover East

I created discovereast.austinwu.com to showcase Duke University's East Campus dormitory rooms to incoming first-year students. At Duke, all first year students and only first year students live on our East Campus, and every fall first year students are always eager to see what their first college dorm room will look like.

While there originally were some photos of East Campus dorm rooms available online, I felt that the flat photos that could not accurately capture any of the rooms and first years would appreciate some more continuous images. This website uses Google's photo sphere technology to render three-dimensional panoramic photos that I took of East Campus dormitory rooms.

Presta

Presta

Presta is a mobile application that connects people with unsued outdoor equipment to people who want to use it, while helping people make cash off their items. Outdoor equipment in particular is very expensive to buy new, and is often only used during specific and limited times - like weekends or days off. Outdoor stores can rent out equipment, but it can often be expensive or far away, making it inconvenient for an individual to borrow outdoor equipment for a short period of time.

Because the cost of buying new outdoor equipment is so high, it can be difficult for people or families to justify making the purchase for a one-time adventure. Presta opens the door for people to go on outdoors trips without spending a lot of money.

Presta was written with a Node.js backend, PostgreSQL database with Knex and Bookshelf.js, and a mobile application built using the Ionic framework with Facebook authentication.

Dumpstr
